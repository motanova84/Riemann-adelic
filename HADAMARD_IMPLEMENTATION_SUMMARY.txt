MATHEMATICAL CONTENT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Main Result:
  If f and g are entire functions of order ≤ 1 with:
    1. Same zeros (with multiplicities)
    2. Agreement at one point
  Then f ≡ g everywhere.

Classical References:
  • Hadamard, J. (1893): "Sur les fonctions entières"
  • Titchmarsh, E.C. (1939): "The Theory of Functions"
  • Boas, R.P. (1954): "Entire Functions"
