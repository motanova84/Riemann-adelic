hilbert_polya_qcal: Si Ĥ_π es auto-adjunto, sus autovalores son reales puros.

Demostración en Lean 4:

theorem hilbert_polya_qcal
  {H : Type*} [NormedAddCommGroup H] [InnerProductSpace ℂ H] [CompleteSpace H]
  (H_π : HamiltonianPi H) (E : ℂ) (ψ : H) (hψ : ψ ≠ 0)
  (h_eigen : H_π.op ψ = E • ψ) :
  E.im = 0 := by
  have h_sa := H_π.is_self_adjoint
  have h_real : ∃ r : ℝ, E = (r : ℂ) := by
    apply IsSelfAdjoint.eigenvalue_isReal
    · exact h_sa
    · exact h_eigen
    · exact hψ
  obtain ⟨r, hr⟩ := h_real
  rw [hr]
  simp
